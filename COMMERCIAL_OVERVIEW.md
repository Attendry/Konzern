# Konzern - Commercial Overview

## Streamline Your Consolidated Financial Statements with HGB Compliance

**Konzern** is a comprehensive web-based solution for preparing consolidated financial statements in accordance with German accounting standards (HGB - Handelsgesetzbuch). Designed for accounting teams and financial professionals, it transforms the complex, time-consuming process of consolidation into an automated, accurate workflow.

### Key Capabilities

**Automated Consolidation Engine**: The platform performs sophisticated consolidation calculations automatically, including intercompany profit elimination, debt consolidation (Forderungen/Verbindlichkeiten), capital consolidation (Kapitalkonsolidierung), and participation book value adjustments. All calculations comply with HGB §§ 290-314.

**Intelligent Company Management**: Manage complex group structures with hierarchical company relationships. The system automatically checks consolidation obligations (Konsolidierungspflicht) based on majority interests, unified management, and control agreements, ensuring regulatory compliance.

**Seamless Data Import**: Import financial data directly from Excel or CSV files, eliminating manual data entry. The system validates account structures and automatically processes multiple sheets and complex formats.

**Comprehensive Financial Statements**: Generate consolidated balance sheets and income statements with full audit trails. The platform handles first-time consolidations (Erstkonsolidierung), calculates goodwill and negative goodwill, and manages minority interests.

**Regulatory Compliance**: Built-in checks ensure adherence to HGB requirements, including consolidation obligation assessments, exception handling, and automatic generation of consolidated notes (Konzernanhang) with all mandatory disclosures.

### Business Value

Reduce consolidation time by up to 80% while eliminating calculation errors. Ensure HGB compliance with automated regulatory checks. Gain real-time visibility into group financial performance through an intuitive dashboard with modern, responsive design.

**Perfect for**: Accounting firms, corporate finance departments, and organizations managing multiple subsidiaries requiring HGB-compliant consolidated reporting.
